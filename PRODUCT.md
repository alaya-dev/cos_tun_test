# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Shoppers in Tunisia browse the public catalogue primarily on mobile, seeking clear product information, availability, and calm guidance before placing a cash-on-delivery order. Store operators use the separate back office to maintain reliable catalogue data.

## Product Purpose

Passion Cosmetic is a French-language cosmetics storefront. Its public experience makes face-free product discovery, category browsing, search, and product selection feel trustworthy and effortless while preserving server-rendered performance and SEO.

## Brand Personality

Elegant, warm, and confident. The experience evokes a calm beauty boutique in daylight: product-led, editorial, practical, and approachable rather than loud or trend-driven.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic dropshipping layouts, pink-only beauty blogs, medical-pharmacy styling, glossy gradients, fake ratings, human-face imagery, intrusive newsletter collection, and client-only storefront navigation.

## Design Principles

1. Let products and useful information lead every decision.
2. Use editorial restraint to create premium character.
3. Balance warm neutrals, deep ink, and selective pink accents.
4. Make commerce actions clear, fast, and predictable.
5. Treat mobile, accessibility, and server-rendered performance as first-class requirements.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.1 AA minimum, support reduced motion, maintain visible keyboard focus and semantic navigation, use meaningful product alt text, and never communicate state through color alone.
